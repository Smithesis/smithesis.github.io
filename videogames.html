Here are some of my favorite games that I would recommend.

Sea of Thieves

This is one of my favorite games. You play as a pirate about a ship that you pilot yourself. You sail around the map, looking for treasure, and fighting other pirates. The gameworld is beutifully designed. Combat can be challenging, but that makes the game even more fun, once you get better at the game and get past getting sunk constantly.
